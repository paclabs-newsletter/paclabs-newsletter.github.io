How We Cracked a Business Problem

The Client

One of India's leading Hire-Train-Staff companies.

The Problem Statement

An integrated workflow involving 4 discrete systems, to register candidates, deliver assessments, and collate performance data.

The Solution

A platform with our test engine as the center piece was integrated with the client website, a third party CRM, and a third party spoken english test engine to enable and automate the following high-level workflow:

  1. Candidate registers on the client website.
  2. Once the candidate is eligible to take the test, he/she is redirected to the PAC engine, which is white-labelled for the client (the candidate believes that he/she is still on the client's platform!)
  3. Post completing the MCQ test, the candidate is provided with credentials to attempt the spoken english test, after which, the test scores are pulled into the PAC engine. Finally, the candidate scores (including the spoken english scores) are transferred to the third party CRM.
